What are the different tiers offered by Reboot?

Reboot offers three tiers: Base Functionality, Premium Functionality, and MVP Functionality. Each tier builds upon the previous one, adding more features and capabilities to suit your needs.

Is Reboot free to use?

Yes, Reboot is free to use with its Base Functionality. However, we offer Premium and MVP versions that include additional features and services for a fee.

Is Reboot open-source?

Yes, Reboot follows an open-source philosophy, allowing developers to contribute and customize the platform to fit their specific needs.

What kind of authentication does Reboot support?

Reboot supports Supabase Authentication for the Base tier and adds Postgres & Back-End Authentication in the Premium tier, ensuring secure user management without extra third-party fees.

Does Reboot include payment integration?

Yes, the Premium tier of Reboot includes Stripe integration for seamless payment processing and subscription management.

How does Reboot help with SEO?

Reboot’s Premium tier includes SEO Integration, providing tools to optimize your app’s content and improve its search engine rankings.

Can I send emails using Reboot?

Yes, the Premium tier includes Mail Sending with MailGun, allowing you to manage and send emails efficiently from your application.

What AI capabilities does Reboot offer?

Reboot's Premium tier includes AI Integration with the OpenAI API, enabling advanced natural language processing and intelligent features in your application.

Can I deploy Reboot anywhere?

Yes, Reboot offers the flexibility to deploy your application anywhere you want, ensuring you can choose the best hosting solution for your needs.
